Unfolding: An Augmented Reality Experience

Holding the world in your hand, you are the sunset. Unfolding is an augmented reality experience created to spark a new perspective of human connection through technology.

Point your mobile device at the QR code, click the yellow pop-up to open the link, and cast your warm light onto the art center we all know and love.
Continue pointing your camera at the QR code while slowly rotating the paper back and forth, setting your sunlight as Oklahoma Contemporary reflects beautiful colors.


TECH SUPPORT: Is the QR link broken or only showing a white circle?
Try using another web browser (Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome) and opening NicholiNoah.com/unfolding/gift.
