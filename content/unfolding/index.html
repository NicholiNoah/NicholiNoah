Unfolding: An Augmented Reality Experience

Holding the world in your hand, you are the sunset. "Unfolding" is an augmented reality experience created to spark new understanding of human connection through technology. Point your mobile device at the QR code, open the link, and cast your warm light onto the art center we all know and love. Slowly rotate the paper left and right to set the sunlight as Oklahoma Contemporary reflects its beautiful color.

There is so much more I'd like to add to this project. Interactivity, a link to the OK Contemp donation page, and some fun stuff that spins.
