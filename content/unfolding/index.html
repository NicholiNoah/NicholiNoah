Unfolding: An Augmented Reality Experience

Holding the world in your hand, you are the sunset. Unfolding is an augmented reality experience created to spark new perspective of human connection through technology.

1. Open your mobile device's camera and point it at the QR code.
2. Click the yellow pop-up on your screen to open the link.
3. Tap "allow" to give Unfolding permission to use your camera.
4. Continue pointing your camera at the QR code (in your other hand) while slowly rotating the paper back and forth.
5. Rise and set your sunlight as Oklahoma Contemporary reflects beautiful colors.


TECH SUPPORT: Is the QR link broken or only showing a white circle?
Using another web browser (Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome), open NicholiNoah.com/unfolding/gift and follow the instructions above.
