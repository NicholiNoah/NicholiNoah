Nicholi Noah (b.1979 Oklahoma City, OK)

NFT (the Frame Stays In the Picture), 2023

Wooden frame on lcd video of animated 3D scan
76 cm x 106 cm (30 in x 42 in), 12 min (looped)


NFT (the Frame Stays In the Picture) is in itself a paradox of concepts.
Non-Fungible animation existing only in virtual form.
as a digital 3D scan of a very fungible real-life wooden frame.


I first exhibited a prototype of this art piece in 2021.
People stood in front of it, staring and taking pictures.
I knew I had a hit, and am re-making a better one.

This time showcasing the frame I found on the side of the road on big trash day.

Previously garbage, now presented as a one-of-a-kind story of its own.
Jettisoned into eternal cyberspace.
Always the same direction, never the same perspective.
