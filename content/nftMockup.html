Nicholi Noah (b.1979 Oklahoma City, OK)

NFT (The Frame Stays In the Picture), 2023

Wooden frame on lcd video display of animated 3D scan
76 cm x 106 cm (30 in x 42 in)

A visualization of an NFT and how it functions in it's intangible world.
NFT (The Frame Stays In the Picture) is itself a paradox of concepts.
Non-Fungible animation existing only in digital form.

Directly tied to, and completely reliant on a very fungible wooden frame.

The frame is real, and the animation is a 3D scan of the real frame.

I first exhibited a prototype of this art piece in 2021.
Viewers stood in front of it, staring then taking pictures.
I knew I had a hit, and am re-making a better one.

Showcasing the frame I found on the side of the road on big trash day.

Previously garbage, now presented as a one-of-a-kind story of its own.
Jettisoned into eternal cyberspace. Same direction, never the same perspective.

This is the third stage of completion for this piece - 1-hour video loop
The next (final) stage will include is own microcomputer
Functioning in 100% real-time code and truly never repeating, forever.
